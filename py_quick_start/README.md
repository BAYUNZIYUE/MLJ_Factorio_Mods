# Pyanodons Quick Start

Pyanodons Quick Start gives new players a small late-start equipment package for
Pyanodons saves.

Scroll down for the Chinese version.

## What It Gives

When the player enters the world with a valid character, the mod gives the
player:

- 100 `py-construction-robot-mk04`
- 1 `power-armor-mk2`
- Equipment inserted into the armor grid:
  - 1 night vision equipment
  - 1 belt immunity equipment
  - 55 solar panel equipment
  - 2 exoskeleton equipment
  - 4 personal roboport MK2 equipment
  - 2 quantum batteries

## When It Runs

- On mod initialization.
- When a player joins the game.
- When a cutscene finishes or is cancelled.

The mod records which players have already received the package, so the same
player should not receive it repeatedly.

## Dependencies

- Required: `base`
- Required: `pyalternativeenergy`
- Optional: `Kux-PersonalTeleport`

# Pyanodons 快速开局

Pyanodons Quick Start 会在 Pyanodons 存档中给新玩家一套开局装备。

## 会给予什么

当玩家进入世界并拥有有效角色时，模组会给予：

- 100 个 `py-construction-robot-mk04`
- 1 件 `power-armor-mk2`
- 并在装甲网格中放入：
  - 1 个夜视模块
  - 1 个锚定模块
  - 55 个太阳能模块
  - 2 个机械外骨骼
  - 4 个机器人指令模块 MK2
  - 2 个量子电池

## 什么时候生效

- 模组初始化时。
- 玩家加入游戏时。
- 过场动画结束或取消时。

模组会记录已经领取过装备的玩家，避免同一玩家重复领取。

## 依赖

- 必需：`base`
- 必需：`pyalternativeenergy`
- 可选：`Kux-PersonalTeleport`
