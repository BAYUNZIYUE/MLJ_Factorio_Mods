Version: 1.1.3
Date: 2026-05-22
  Changes:
    - 修复删除太空平台后，物流编组轮询访问已失效飞船缓存导致的不可恢复错误。
    - 当缓存批次发现已删除飞船时会跳过该对象并请求重建缓存，避免后续 tick 继续访问失效平台。

Version: 1.1.2
Date: 2026-05-11
  Changes:
    - 太空平台枢纽品质切换改为动态读取当前启用的品质链，支持扩展品质模组。
    - 太空平台枢纽达到最高品质后不再回到最低品质。

    - Space platform hubs no longer wrap back to the lowest quality after reaching the highest quality.
    - Changed the ship simulation multiplier to "quality level + 1", making vanilla quality multipliers 1/2/3/4/6.

Version: 1.0.3
Date: 2025-05-04
  Changes:
    - 【重要】优化飞船投放逻辑，现基本移除返还机制，改为投放数个相同的火箭下去.
    - 修复误判火箭包含玩家而导致没有应用倍率修改的bug.
    - 修复误判是否从飞船发射火箭而导致物品处理错误的bug.
    - 对于普通品质的飞船，不再处理与它们相关的火箭，从而大幅提升性能.
    - 飞船的倍数不再使用1 2 3 4 6，而是1 2 3 4 5，以方便货物数目的计算.
    - [Important] Optimize spaceship launching logic, now basically removes the return mechanism and replaces it with launching several identical rockets.
    - Fixed a bug that caused the multiplier modification to not be applied due to misjudging if the rocket contains a player.
    - Fixed a bug that incorrectly determined if a rocket was launched from an airship, resulting in incorrect item handling.
    - For normal quality airships, the rockets associated with them are no longer processed, resulting in a significant performance increase.
    - The multiplier for airships no longer uses 1 2 3 4 6, but rather 1 2 3 4 5, to make it easier to calculate the number of cargoes.
